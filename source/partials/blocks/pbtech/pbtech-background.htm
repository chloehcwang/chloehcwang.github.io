The Background


This is a given context design challenge.

Diana and Alan, owners of a small business, used the PB Tech website to buy new laptops (about 15 units), but they were dissatisfied with the online shopping experience.

The task is to use the given story as the context, provide a prototype for the development of redesigned laptop feature pages. Using https://www.pbtech.co.nz/category/computers/laptops as a reference..
