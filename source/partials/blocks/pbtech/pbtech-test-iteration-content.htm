Testing Our Assumptions


Once the team had a prototype ready for use, we knew we needed to put it in the hands of our customers.

Usability Test

Methodology : Interview

Participant: 5 Users

Results: The test helps us verify 2 things we did well and find 3 issues to improve.

Key






Findings

  • Things we did well

    • 100% of Participants were able to successfully complete the quiz.


    Verified hybrid approach can meet users’ “berry-picking model”.

    The solution of hybrid approach works well in our user test. Three of them started with the quiz at the very beginning. The other two users, one started with browsing the most popular laptops and the other one used the search bar directly.

  • Things need to be improved

    • According to the discussion of Severity Rating, the problems below became our priority to fix.

    Iteration

    One selected iteration example

    Redesigned up-selling information for corporate customer

    So, we changed the wording of the CTA from "apply for business account" to "contact us".

    Signing up is a big commitment for most people. Most users today are more wary than ever about how to handle their personal information.

    Before applying for an account, they need to know more information to have trust and comfort. By clicking contact us , it will lead to PB-tech business service page. So the user can get access to the information they need regarding PB-tech's business plan and contact methods.

    We added the business information on the landing page by placing it in the hero area. Because one of our testers, Janet, who had real-world experience purchasing computers for her company, had a tendency to go to the home page rather than the product page.
