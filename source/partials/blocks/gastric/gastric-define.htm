Problem Statement ONE

The existing gastric app is being used in adults designed to track and rate the severity of their gastric symptoms, however the app is not suitable for paediatric use (5-18 years old).
Because children have a different level of health literacy, they are confused when they see a scale to rate symptoms. They are also bored with the lack of engagement within the app.
As a result, the child may not fully understand making the ratings inaccurate.

Problem Statement TWO

The current app has no ability to track symptoms in the week leading up to clinic day. This is important information as the patient could be having a “good” day when they come in for the clinic session.
Because of this, the participant tries to recall the symptoms they have had for the week and it is more difficult for children to remember.
As a result, it is unlikely the researcher can get an accurate inforamtion during the 7 days leading up to clinic day, so the diagnosis may be inaccurate.
