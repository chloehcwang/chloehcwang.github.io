The brief

Our client wants to develop a paediatrics version of Alimetryᵀᴹ to help doctors diagnose. Gastric Alimetryᵀᴹ technology non-invasively senses the motility of the stomach to detect the underlying mechanisms of gastric symptoms, informing personalized therapy.

The task is to redsigned the severity app for children, aged 5-17 years. To enable a young person to record gastric symptoms once a day, for 7 days before clinic day for further testing.

The APP needs to be clear and simple for the little ones to use. The information data can be captured and recorded for research analyses. The app should be based on the adult version with the same logo and branding colour.

Gastric Alimetryᵀᴹ

is a non-invasive device, worn in the comfort of the clinic.
The device delivers new validated biomarkers of gastric function, based on multi-award-winning scientific innovation.

The challenge

Coping techniques to help kids thrive

  • Adult app currently not suitable and engaging for paediatric use
  • Children struggle to understand how to rate pain severity using a 0-10 scale
  • Children find it diificult to understand the difference between all 10 symptoms
  • Design an app that is engaging for 5-8 age group, but not boring for 9-14 age group

My role
